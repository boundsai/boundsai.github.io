Technology & Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (BUTAI LAB)

Research Focus

We develop state-of-the-art AI solutions across high-impact application areas. Our work includes realistic behavior modeling using super-agent-based algorithms and multi-agent reinforcement learning (RL) in distributed systems to train AI pilots for tactical military scenarios. These AI pilots can interact with human pilots across air, ground, and naval missions.

We also enrich tactical environment simulators with RL by integrating large language models (LLMs), enabling virtual entities to adapt to strategic commands and discover new skills and complex strategies. In healthcare, we build multimodal AI models supported by molecular biomarkers to detect and subtype breast cancer from multiple imaging modalities—serving as an early-stage, second-look assistant for radiologists.

In agriculture, we use RL to optimize plasma-activated water (PAW) production as a natural pesticide alternative that adapts to changing environmental conditions. Finally, in education, we develop a smart PTZ camera system that uses RL to capture meaningful classroom scenes and LLMs to produce high-quality lecture notes to support learning.
