Why pursue a Data Science
& Artificial Intelligence degree?

At our department, we focus on both the theoretical foundations and practical applications of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence.
Our research spans a wide range of core areas, including machine learning, statistics, deep learning, computer vision, reinforcement learning, generative AI, and natural
language processing. We are also deeply engaged in interdisciplinary research, applying data driven and AI based approaches across diverse domains such as medicine,
finance, the defense industry, and beyond.
