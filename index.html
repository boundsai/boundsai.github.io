Research Labs

At the Institute for Data Science & Artificial Intelligence, we aim to bring together experts across different disciplines to foster interdisciplinary collaborations. Our core and affiliated members conduct fundamental and applied research on artificial intelligence and big data technologies. We seek solutions to problems in fields as diverse as health, finance, engineering and basic sciences by harnessing the power of data.

    • Autonomous Agents Lab

      Research on reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, planning, and decision-making for autonomous robots and software agents.

    • Computational Linguistics Lab

      Natural language processing and language technologies: parsing, information extraction, multilingual NLP, and data-driven language modeling.

    • Control & Intelligent Systems Lab

      Control theory and intelligent systems: robotics, optimization, system identification, and learning-based control for complex dynamical systems.

    • Özönder Lab

      Physics-inspired modeling and machine learning, with an emphasis on robust inference, computation, and scientific discovery.

    • Technology & Artificial Int. Lab

      Applied AI and systems: computer vision, deep learning applications, intelligent platforms, and real-world AI deployment.
