Research Labs

At the Institute for Data Science & Artificial Intelligence, we aim to bring together experts across different disciplines to foster interdisciplinary collaborations. Our core and affiliated members conduct fundamental and applied research on artificial intelligence and big data technologies. We seek solutions to problems in fields as diverse as health, finance, engineering and basic sciences by harnessing the power of data.

    • Autonomous Agents Lab

    • Leveraging advanced sensor technologies like 3D Lidar, thermal imaging, millimeter wave radar and depth sensing, we create intelligent systems that navigate and interact seamlessly with their environments.

      Learn more →

    • Computational Linguistics Lab

    • We develop advanced models and tools to analyze language, from historical texts to modern applications, bridging technology and linguistics.

      Learn more →

    • Control & Intelligent Systems Lab

    • Control, optimization and artificial intelligence methodologies for a wide range of problems whose solutions may have a huge societal impact. Learn more →

    • Özönder Lab

    • AI agents, large language models, computer vision, AI in finance, AI-accelerated drug discovery and materials discovery, industrial applications of AI.

      Learn more →

    • Technology & Artificial Int. Lab

    • Developing realistic behaviour models using super-agent-based algorithms and multi-agent reinforcement learning in distributed systems to train AI pilots for tactical military scenarios.

      Learn more →
